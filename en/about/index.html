About · Privacy Policy

A free suite of PDF tools with no install and no sign-up. The biggest difference: your files are never uploaded to a server.

Every task in Everything PDF runs only inside your web browser (on your own device). No file is ever sent to a server.

How it works

How are my files processed?

Merging, splitting, converting, and every other task runs only inside your web browser (on your own device). Your PDFs are never uploaded to any server, and once a task finishes or you close the tab, they disappear from memory. Once you've loaded the page, most features even keep working with no internet connection.

Privacy

Do you collect any personal data?

This site has no sign-up and collects or stores no personal data at all — no files, no email, nothing. Because files are never sent anywhere, uploaded documents are never kept off your device. That said, third parties such as Google may use cookies to display ads (see 'Ads & cookies' below).

Ads · cookies

How are ads and cookies used?

To stay free, this site displays Google AdSense ads. Third-party ad vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can turn off personalized ads in Google Ads Settings, or opt out of third-party ad cookies altogether at aboutads.info. Regardless of ads or analytics, the PDF files you add are still never sent to a server and are processed only in your browser.

Pricing

Is it free?

Yes. Everything is completely free — no watermarks, no limits on file count or size, and no sign-up required.

About unlock

About the unlock tool

Unlock is not a cracking tool for figuring out passwords; it only removes a password you already know, or print/edit restrictions. Please use it only on your own documents that you're authorized to modify.
