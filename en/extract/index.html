    This is the tool for when you need to pull out only certain pages. Enter the page numbers you want, and just those pages are gathered into one new PDF. Perfect for submitting a specific clause of a contract or a particular chapter of a report.

    How to use

    Extract PDF Pages How to use

    1. Upload your PDF.
    2. Enter the pages to extract (e.g., 1, 3, 5-7).
    3. Preview to confirm the pages come out in the order you entered.
    4. Click Extract to save them as a new PDF.
    Your files are never uploaded to any server.Every extraction happens inside your browser — on your device only. Sensitive documents like contracts or official paperwork never leave your hands, so use it with confidence.
    Note

    Great for moments like these

    Sending someone just the few pages of a contract that hold the key clause, pulling out only the chapter you were assigned from a thick report, or setting aside just the pages you need from an application or official document. It works the same way on scanned PDFs — pick out exactly the pages you want.

    Note

    Good to know when entering pages

    Enter page numbers based on the original PDF. If you enter a number that doesn't exist (like 25 in a 20-page document), that part simply won't be extracted. And if you list the same page more than once, it'll be included that many times — so use the result preview to double-check before saving and avoid slip-ups.

    Frequently asked questions

    Frequently asked questions

    Q1Can I pull out just one specific page from a PDF?
    Yes — just enter the page number you want, and only that page is extracted. If you only need page 3 of a 30-page report, for example, simply enter '3.' None of the other pages make it into the result file.
    Q2Can I extract several ranges at once?
    Absolutely. Enter pages and ranges together separated by commas, like '1, 3, 5-7,' and they're all handled in one pass. Feel free to mix scattered single pages with continuous ranges.
    Q3Does extraction keep the page order?
    It follows exactly the order you type. Enter '5, 1, 3' and the result is saved as page 5, then 1, then 3. You can even use this to rearrange pages into a different order than the original.
    Q4How do I save each page as a separate file?
    This tool gathers your chosen pages into a single PDF. If you'd rather peel pages off individually into separate files, the 'Split PDF' tool is a better fit. Use this extract tool when you want to collect a selection into one file.
    Q5Are my files uploaded to a server? Is it free?
    Your files are never uploaded — everything is processed on your device only. It's free to use with no sign-up or install, and there are no watermarks or limits on file count or size.
