      PDF Security 4

      Lock or unlock with a password, strip personal data, and protect with watermarks.

      Password Protect PDFSet an open password so only people who know it can open the PDF. You can also restrict printing and copying. It's the opposite of Unlock, and everything is encrypted right in your browser, with nothing ever uploaded. Unlock PDFFree up a password-protected PDF. Uses a password you already know and works entirely on your device. Remove PDF Personal InfoWipe every hidden document property (title, author, creator app, and more) to erase traces of personal info. The content stays as-is. WatermarkStamp text like 'CONFIDENTIAL' or 'COPY', or a logo image, onto every page. Place it diagonally in the center or tile it across the page, and fine-tune the angle and opacity to deter unauthorized copying.

      Compress PDFShrink PDFs packed with scans or photos. Ideal for fitting email attachments or the size limits on upload sites. Remove Blank Pages from PDFAutomatically find and remove blank pages mixed into a scan, then save a new PDF. Great for clearing the empty backs of double-sided scans. Convert PDF to GrayscaleTurn a color PDF into black and white. Shrinks the file and suits grayscale printing. Text may become an image and no longer be selectable.

      Polish your document with rotation, margins, cropping, signatures, and form flattening.

      Rotate PDFStraighten sideways scans and upside-down documents by rotating left or right 90° or 180°, all at once. Text and quality stay untouched. Crop PDF (Remove Margins)Trim wide margins on every side (a little, medium, or a lot) to fit the content. Text and quality are fully preserved. Add Margins to PDFAdd white margins around every page. Great for binding room, note-taking space, or preventing edge cut-off. Sign a PDFDraw your signature, upload a stamp or signature image, or type your name, then drag it wherever you want on the preview. Because nothing is ever uploaded, signing contracts and consent forms stays completely private. Flatten PDF FormLock the values in a filled-out application or contract onto the page so recipients can't change them and it looks identical everywhere. Pages aren't turned into images, so the text stays crisp.
